Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Drag colored blocks from your containers onto the grid. Line up three or more of the same color to clear them. Keep placing blocks next to matching colors to build your streak and multiply your score.

What is the Daily Challenge?
Every day, all players get the same board in a two-minute time attack. Your score is submitted to a global leaderboard that resets each day. Come back tomorrow for a fresh board.

How do the leaderboards work?
Brixl uses Apple's Game Center. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center (Settings > Game Center) to submit scores and see the global rankings.

My progress disappeared / a score didn't save.
Progress is stored on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, local progress may be lost. Game Center scores are tied to your Apple ID.

I found a bug.
Please email us with your device model and iOS version, and a description of what happened. It really helps us fix things quickly.
