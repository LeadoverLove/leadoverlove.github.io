Brixl — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03/07/2026 at 08:27am

LeadoverLove ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the Brixl app ("the App") does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves your game progress and preferences (such as settings, high scores, and streaks) locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Game Center

If you are signed in to Apple's Game Center, the App submits your game scores to leaderboards so you can compete with other players. This is handled entirely by Apple's Game Center service and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your Game Center identity.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
